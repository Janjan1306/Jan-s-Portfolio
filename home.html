Flavours of home / Vị nhà
2D animation
Group project
2025
In the 2D animated film, a humanized otter girl prepares a supper for herself while recalling her early culinary experiences with her mother. She experiences a range of emotions during the session as a result of the recollections, which gives her drive and delight as she treasures each and every happy event she shared with her mother as a child. Through its portrayal of Vietnamese traditional culture, the cartoon highlights the value of family meals and the country's cultural legacy. It also values the bonds that are formed between family members through regular family meals.
